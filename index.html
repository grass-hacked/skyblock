THE ULTIMATE GUIDE TO COMBAT RUSHING SKYBLOCK.

Hypixel Skyblock is a game of multiple genres, combat, farming, mining, fishing and more but some players here just combat rush to Floor seven of the catacombs, while you grind hours trying to increase your stats to be 1-shot in floor 5. That raises the debate though combat rushers start having difficulty past floor 6 is the time that they used to get there worth it? Yes, it is. Now without further ado read on to the guide for combat rushing in Hypixel skyblock.

First thing any player must do for combat rushing is unlocking the end, it is a vital early game money making method, which aids in buying dungeon gear for starting out in them.the end is unlocked at combat 8, until then it is smart to either farm in the deep caverns or spiders den. In my opinion killing the highest hp mob in the deep caverns is a better choice as players also mine and earn some money to get some grinding gear for the end.

To start any player should try and unlock the farming islands, this is easily achieved by farming wheat on the hub. Now I know that this is not sounding like a combat rushing guide but without this small ammount of other skills most combat islands are locked. So, after mining wheat and unlocking the farming islands, one must mine sand and increase their mining level so they can begin killing zombies for exp in the deep caverns. Once the deep caverns are unlocked you can forget any skill but combat. In the deep caverns try and kill the mobs on the lowest floor that you can one tap and not be killed by. Do this for a while till you unlock the spiders den.

Once in the spiders den try and find a lobby where people do arachne a lot, do it with them, just hit a few shots on arachne and wait
